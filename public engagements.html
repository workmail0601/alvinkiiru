Public Engagements

Metis ReimaginED 2023

22nd - 24th December 2023 | The National Museum of Kenya Volunteer Management and Site Visit Lead.

I was contracted by Metis collective to assist in the recruitment and management of volunteers for the third edition of the ReimaginED Summit. We recruited 15 high capacity volunteers who were put under my direction. I developed an intricate 2 day schedule for volunteer stationing to maximize capacities and minimize the numbers required - leading to a well balanced budget. I also managed an experiential site visit to SHOFCO Kibera School for Girls (23rd of December 2023), where I led a group of guests in learning from the school on how they are implementing the country’s new CBC model of learning considering financial constraints. I also co-led an awards session where we recognized 8 changemakers in education and paid tribute to a recently fallen member of the Metis team.

Metis ElevatED 2023

1st April 2023 | CFK - Binti Pamoja HQs Presentation on Launch of the new Metis Alumni platform

Part of my duties in my role as a member of the Metis Alumni Council were to make a presentation on the new platform (Mighty Networks) designed to empower the alumni to work together in a more cohesive fashion. I designed the presentation with my InDesign skills, and made a 20 minute presentation to the alumni focusing on value from their perspective. We managed to get 85% of the alumni to register for the platform during and immediately after the session, while at the same time paving the way for the bigger platform launch later that same year.

Metis ReimaginED 2022

9th December 2022 | The National Museum of Kenya Climate Resilience Conversation Co-Lead

I co-led a conversation on behalf of Nikola Energy on climate change together with Amisa Rashid (Nivishe Foundation), Grace Wanene (Drawing Dreams Initiative) and Sheena Shah (Harvesting for Good East Africa), its effects on the societal level and relevant contextualized climate change solutions. ReimaginED was an event hosted by Metis collective in a bid to bring key stakeholders in education and climate change together.

Projects Led

Improving the Quality of Education in Low Income areas (Community of Practice)

July - August 2024

My role at Metis collective as an Alumni Consultant allowed me to design and execute a community of practice (COP) session on understanding the barriers to improving the quality of education in low income areas (Kibera being the sample area). We mobilized Metis proximate leaders in education from the area and over a course of 2 months, had discussions on the challenges they faced in their quest to improve education, and the possible solutions to aforementioned challenges.

The Automated Handwashing System

March - June 2020

In my internship period at Nikola Energy, I managed a project together with students from Africa Nazarene University to develop the first fully independent automated handwashing system to help in curbing the spread of COVID-19, by increasing access to safe handwashing services.
