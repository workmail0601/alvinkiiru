Fahari Innovations Challenge

Podium Finish 24th November 2021

The Fahari Innovations Challenge was designed by Kenya Airways in partnership with Qhala, SNDBX, Embraer, Safaricom Business and the Ministry of Tourism Kenya. It was a 3 month event where innovators in Kenya got to showcase their ideas and application to the airline industry. I went there on behalf of the Partnership between Nikola Energy, where I work and Omiflo Kenya, and presented our idea on sustainable sewage management, an idea which came runners up in the challenge on the 24th of November 2021.
